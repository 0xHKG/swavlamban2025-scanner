## üé® Design Guidelines

### Color Palette (Consistent Across All Assets)
```
Primary Colors:
- Navy Blue: #0D47A1 (backgrounds, main elements)
- Gold/Yellow: #FFD700 (accents, year)
- White: #FFFFFF (text, contrast)

Secondary Colors:
- Brown: #8B4513 (QR codes, accents)
- Beige: #F5DEB3 (QR backgrounds)
- Navy Blue: #1D4E89 (QR codes)
- Gray: #D3D3D3 (QR backgrounds)
- Green: #2E7D32 (Panel 2 QR)
- Light Green: #C8E6C9 (Panel 2 QR background)
```

### Typography
- **Titles**: Sans-serif, bold, large (48-72pt)
- **Subtitles**: Sans-serif, medium (24-36pt)
- **Body Text**: Sans-serif, regular (14-18pt)
- **Small Text**: Sans-serif, regular (10-12pt)

### Branding Elements (Include on All)
- Indian Navy emblem
- Government of India emblem
- NIIO logo (if available)
- Event sponsors (if any)

### QR Code Placement (IMPORTANT)
- **Position**: Left side of pass
- **Left Margin**: 60 pixels from edge
- **Vertical**: Centered vertically
- **Sizes**:
  - Exhibition passes: 220 x 220 px
  - Session passes: 400 x 400 px
- **Border/Quiet Zone**: 5px around QR code
- **Leave space blank** - QR codes generated by system
